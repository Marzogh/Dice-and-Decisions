⚔️ Turn Flow

✅ The usual turn▶
  1. Move (up to 30 ft).
  2. Action: attack, hide, help, dodge, dash, disengage, search, use an item.
  3. Bonus action: at Rogue 2, Cunning Action (Dash / Disengage / Hide).
  4. Reaction: one per round (later: Uncanny Dodge / Silvery Barbs).
🎲 Advantage / disadvantage▶

Advantage = roll 2d20, keep the higher. Disadvantage = roll 2d20, keep the lower.

  • Being hidden usually gives advantage on your first attack.
  • Faerie Fire can create advantage (character level 3).
  • Multiple sources don’t stack; you either have it or you don’t.
🗡️ Sneak Attack▶

Once per turn, when you hit with a finesse or ranged weapon and:

  • You have advantage, or
  • An ally is within 5 ft of the target and you don’t have disadvantage.

Damage 1d6 extra damage at Rogue 1. Increases at higher rogue levels.

🧠 Spell Save DC▶

Some spells don’t roll to hit. Instead the target rolls a saving throw to resist.

Spell Save DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + spellcasting modifier

  • Arcane Trickster modifier = INT (currently −1).
  • Drow lineage spell modifier = CHA (currently +3).

Build note Low INT DC favours utility, positioning, deception, and “no-save” value.
